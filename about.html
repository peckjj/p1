About Us

Welcome to the Rockville Youth Soccer Association's main website! RYSA is a non-profit recreational soccer league dedicated to providing a healthy form of activity to children of all ages in the Rockville community. The organization is entirely led by volunteers, and all are welcome to lend a hand and participate in exciting community events! The official start of the Spring 2021 season begins April 5th, so be sure to sign up!

The RYSA Mission

In all ways, the RYSA seeks to foster and uphold 3 core values:

Safety

  • The strictest safety guidelines will be followed for all events sponsored by the RYSA at all times. The RYSA will not tolerate or allow any unsafe behavior.

Sportsmanship

  • The RYSA celebrates humility in victory, and grace in defeat. Out of respect for the sport of Soccer, the RYSA will not accept unsportsmanlike behavior by children, guardians, or any individual.

Inclusion

  • The RYSA aims to bring people in the community together. All individuals are welcome, and all children between the ages of 4 and 14 are eligible to join a team. Coaches will be instructed to ensure that all players on their roster are included and participate.

These three values, in order by priority, define the nature and purpose of our organization. Please help us to maintain a positive environment at the RYSA.
